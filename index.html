What is a Mango?

Mango (Mangifera indica) is a tropical fruit native to South Asia, loved worldwide for its sweet, juicy flesh and vibrant color.

Nutritional Value

Mangoes are rich in Vitamin C, Vitamin A, fiber, and antioxidants, making them a delicious and healthy part of a balanced diet.

Life Cycle

From seed germination to flowering, fruiting, and harvest, mango trees take years to mature but reward us with abundant fruit.
