What is a Mango?

Mango (Mangifera indica) is a tropical drupe loved for its sweet aroma, juicy flesh, and sunny color.

Nutritional value

Mangoes offer vitamin C, provitamin A carotenoids, fiber, and natural sweetness that fits a balanced diet.

Life cycle

From seed to fruit: germination, vegetative growth, flowering, fruit set, development, maturity, and harvest.
